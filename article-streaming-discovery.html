Have Streaming Platforms Improved the Way People Discover New Music?

Saturday, December 28, 2025 • Alexandre Hacques

Nowadays, streaming platforms have improved a lot. For many people, it's a new way to discover music.

For example, these platforms give us personalized recommendations through music we have already listened to. It can give us different songs with the same theme or even other types of songs which are still related to what we are listening to. Moreover, it offers the access to a global catalog. So if someone is a fan of Taylor Swift, this person can listen to the newest album without waiting days or weeks to buy it in a shop. The difference is that it is way faster and it is free.

Besides, people from all over the world can create curated playlists and then share them with everybody. This can highlight new artists who don't get mainstream attention. It's incredibly easy to share music with friends through links or social media.

Streaming platforms have made it easier to discover music and richer by suggesting new artists and giving instant access to a huge variety of music.
